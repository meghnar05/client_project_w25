Tutoring at the University of Michigan

The Academic Success Team at UMSI provides resources to help students excel both in and out of the classroom, including peer tutoring for UMSI, programming, and math courses, as well as additional academic support.

What types of tutoring can I receive?

Peer tutoring at the University of Michigan is a free, appointment based program. You can receive support in:

  • Mathematics
  • Programming

Mathematics Tutoring

Classes you can receive help from in mathematics:

  • Linear Algebra
  • Statistics
  • General math related course support

Programming Tutoring

Classes you can receive help from in programming:

  • SI 106: Programs, Information, and People
  • SI 206: Data-Oriented Programming
  • SI 506: Programming I
  • SI 507: Intermediate Programming
  • General Python support

Additional Academic Support

Sweetland Writing Center
Tutoring and courses to support writing at all levels, including multilingual learners.
English Language Institute
Offers academic English courses, GSI training, and TESOL prep for U-M students and staff.
Math Lab
Free tutoring for math courses through 217, plus help for others when possible.
Services for Students with Disabilities (SSD)
Supports students with disabilities by promoting inclusive academic access and success.
